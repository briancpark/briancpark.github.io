안녕! Hi, my name is Brian Park. I am from Charlotte, North Carolina and currently a junior at University of California, Berkeley. Go Bears! I am a proud Korean-American. Here is a link to my resume if you are interested in recruiting me. Scroll down more to know more about me!

Why computer science AND chemistry? I initially chose to major in chemistry because I was interested in working in the hardware tech industry, such as making batteries, screens, and cameras. But after being a student in Berkeley for over a year, I was influenced by the huge wave of computer science students and Bay Area tech companies. So I fast tracked my CS curriculum and took all the requirements to declare CS major at UC Berkeley. I had the intent to switch out of the chemistry major, but realized that I could use my software to solve my difficult chemistry problems and understandings. With the combination of the two curriculum, I am interested in software engineering, artificial intelligence, and quantum computing. Hopefully, quantum supremacy is near and we can change explore new domains of software and hardware in the near future.
