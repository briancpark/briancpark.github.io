Why computer science AND chemistry? I initially chose to major in chemistry because I was interested in working in the hardware tech industry, such as making batteries, screens, and cameras. But after being a student in Berkeley for over a year, I was influenced by the huge wave of Computer Science students and Bay Area tech companies. I fast tracked my CS degree and took all the requirements to declare Computer Science BA major in just two semesters. My interests career wise is software engineering. Paired up with my Chemistry BS major, I am interested in quantum computing when that becomes mainstream. Let's hope that I can develop distributable software for quantum computers in the very near future!
