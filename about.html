About Me

Hello world! My name is Brian Park (he/him/his), and I am a Machine Learning Compiler Engineer at

After being an undergraduate student in Berkeley for over a year, I was highly influenced by the huge wave of computer science students and Bay Area tech companies. So I fast tracked my CS curriculum in two semesters. I enjoy the limitless possibilities with software. Growing up from Charlotte, North Carolina, I don't think I would have ever transitioned into tech without studying and experiencing life in the Bay Area.

Passionate about accelerating machine learning. Doing everything I can to bridge the gap between software and hardware, because at the end of the day, software is the only limitation of what hardware can do.

I did a Computer Science Bachelor's at University of California, Berkeley. I have been part of RISELab/Sky Computing, where I worked on the NumS Project!

I did a Computer Science Master's at College of Engineering, North Carolina State University. I was advised under Xipeng Shen and associated with PICTure Research Group/NCSU Systems Lab.
