About Me

Hello world! My name is Brian Park and I am a computer science student at University of California, Berkeley.

After being a student in Berkeley for over a year, I was influenced by the huge wave of computer science students and Bay Area tech companies. So I fast tracked my CS curriculum in two semesters. I enjoy the limitless possibilities with software.

I am mainly interested in software engineering, machine learning, systems, computer architecture, and high performance computing. Passionate about accelerating machine learning. As of now I am doing research in the RISELab at UC Berkeley on the NumS Project! I will be interning at Apple for a Software Engineering position in the Core OS - Vector and Numerics Group this summer.

Starting Fall 2022, I will be a computer science master's student at North Carolina State University in College of Engineering.
